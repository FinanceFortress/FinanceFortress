About Us

We are a team of personal finance experts dedicated to providing you with the best advice, resources and tools to achieve financial wellness.


Our mission is to help you manage your finances, make smart decisions, and build a sustainable financial future. We understand that personal finance can be complex, which is why we focus on providing simple and straightforward solutions to help you achieve your financial goals.

Tips for budgeting

  • Create a monthly budget and track your income and expenses.
  • Indicate how much of your income should be allocated to major categories such as food, housing, transportation, entertainment, etc.
  • Emphasize the importance of having a small contingency fund.

Tips for planning expenses

  • Define your financial goals and create a plan to achieve each of them.
  • Consider implementing the "50/30/20" system, where 50% of income goes to basic expenses, 30% to personal needs, and 20% to savings and debt repayment.
  • Offer strategies to effectively manage smart purchases and avoid overspending.

Investment advice

  • Explain the benefits of long-term investing and talk about different types of investments such as stocks, bonds, funds, etc.
  • Provide the basic principles of portfolio diversification and the importance of risk allocation.
  • Offer recommendations for finding qualified financial advisors or self-investing resources.

Tips for saving and earning

  • Consider different savings strategies, such as retirement funds, deposits, investment accounts, and more.
  • Suggest ideas for additional income, such as freelancing, distributed work or passive income.
  • Discuss the importance of smart debt management and provide advice on paying off debt.
